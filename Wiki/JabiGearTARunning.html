JabiGear: The Adventure Speedrunning

This page is for speedruns that take advantage of a glitch in both Jabiveir: The Adventure and JabiGear 2 (1): Solid Veir, involving both games' use of the same cookie to track level progress, allowing for level progression in one game to translate into the other; this results in massive shortcuts being made available in both games by switching to the other game to play an easier level. This glitch has been removed from the normal versions of both games, but it has been preserved in these links to old J:TA and old JabiGear.

-The glitch that this speedrunning category depends on was only discovered two years after the release of the relevant games; I found it while writing the Wiki pages for them.
